Starting 2018, Grad Cohort Workshops for Indian women are being conducted by ACM-W India. These Workshops aim to reach out to Indian women graduate students in the field of computing; facilitating interactions between eminent female researchers in the field, from academia and industry, and current students. Here, through sharing some of their own experiences, women experts guide students towards a productive graduate tenure. Some questions that are likely to be discussed are how to choose a mentor, choice of research areas and questions, challenges and perseverance, honing one’s skills, scientific writing and presentation, work-life balance, spirit of collaboration, applying for postdocs/jobs, career options, and much more. In 2022, the ACM-W India Grad Cohort Workshop is being organized in the online mode by IIT Jodhpur. Applicants can look forward to presentations on a host of research areas, mentoring & brainstorming sessions, networking opportunities, and lots more... Important Dates Registration Deadline: July 18, 2022 Workshop: July 21-22, 2022 Eligibility Masters and Ph.D. women students at Indian Universities or Institutes.
